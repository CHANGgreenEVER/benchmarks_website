Boxy, the Unsupervised Llamas, and the Bosch Small Traffic Lights Dataset were all created as part of research within Bosch's automated driving team.
Bosch's automated driving team covers research and development in sensor hardware and software, perception, sensor fusion, localization, mapping, testing, simulation, prediction, behavior generation, trajectory planning, and motion planning. As shown here, scientific publications are possible as part of working on pushing the state-of-the-art in automated driving.
